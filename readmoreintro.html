About-Me

I'm Reema Derawi,20 years old, This is my Blog welcome to you with me I start capturing visual stories and adore bright, clean and uplifting imagery that emphasises the beauty of real life from the circle of camera. my start was in College when the Teacher gave us assigment about "Design Website", I remember that i was so afraid also lake of self-confidence ,but that didn't stop me to go forward and from my lucky i got full mark in that assigment , then I spent months Googling how i should to start , i was really confused about "what i want?" and "what i like?", After that i decided to start in something, I had study two courses about "how to build web-site" and thankfully i find my self now building my own website...
Camera is simply a space where photographers and creatives can find their next step to great photography, Over the last few years I’ve made quite a few valuable friendships with creatives here in this online space. We all have the same doubts and fears, the same challenges and hurdles. The same desire for achievement and connection. Yet not all of us have a support network around us. I am hoping you can find that in the Cadirosa Blog. Photography isn’t a destination, it’s a journey. It’s like fashion, it’s never finished.
